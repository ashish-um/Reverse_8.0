About reverse

a

In the heart of Pune, lies an incubator for poetry- a sacred space where truth is witnessed in its absolute form.

ReVerse is a poetry slam, annually organised by the Magazine Board of the Army Institute of Technology, Pune. First conducted in 2015, it was held 5 times before the lockdown, once after it, and has got another edition coming up April, 2024.

ReVerse has witnessed several awe-inspiring, mountain-moving, spine-chilling, hair-raising and life-changing performances.

Why Participate?

To write poetry is to find meaning, find reasons. And only to speak up is to take the smallest action. What lies on sheets of paper appeals, but only what is said out loud, can make a change. For the days we spend are only counted by what we leave behind, we must strive to etch our voice in the stone of passing time.

What better way there is to make a difference in people's lives and change the world for the better, piece by piece, than by contributing a verse at ReVerse 7.0?
